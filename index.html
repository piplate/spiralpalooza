“To celebrate together what we have made together!”
— Donna King, Founder, Children First

We hope this celebration will mark this moment in time and acknowledge the passing of time — reminding us that two things are equally true: Children First happened and it was important, and Children First is ending because nothing lasts forever.

We hope it will hold space for both gratitude and grief, invite reflection and remembering, help old friends reconnect, and allow us all to savor the pleasure of laying eyes on each other and being together in real life. And be SO MUCH FUN!

What will it be like?

We are structuring Spiralpalooza to contain all the essential ingredients of a Children First experience…

  • Lots of open time for play — with just ONE must-do
  • Beautiful materials at the ready
  • Free flow between indoor and outdoor spaces
  • Lots of sensory play
  • A big space for active play (we’re hoping to close the street for trikes, balls, chase and wild happy play!)
  • Music and dancing
  • A “Here We Are Together” Magic Circle moment — gathering and hearing every Children Firster’s voice
  • Lots of simple and yummy snacks (before the Magic Circle)
  • Pizza dinner and dessert after the Magic Circle
✎ Must-Do: Make a Portfolio Page
Come to the Studio to make a page for the Children First Spiralpalooza Portfolio — share a reflection, a memory, a gratitude, a lingering question, or a wish for the future.
★ Must-Do: The Magic Circle
Join us for the Magic Circle, where every Children Firster is invited to share one memory!
